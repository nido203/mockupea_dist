Frequently Asked Questions

What is Mockupea?

Mockupea is an app for creating a 3d book or magazine mockup in your browser. You can use it to present your new ebook or magazine.


How to use Mockupea?

Use your left mouse button to rotate the view, right button to pan, and mouse wheel to zoom in or out.

To start creating your mockup click on the blue Edit button. First select the type for your mockup (Hardcover or Paperback). After that, you can upload your front, back and spine designs. You can also change color of the spine or remove the uploaded texture by pressing the button "Clear". Tweak the rest of the options to get the desired look.

Furthermore, in the Lighting tab, you can change the background color and play with the light setup, exposure, and ambient light. Once you are happy with your result, find the camera angle you wish to export in Cameras tab and then in Export tab click on PNG or JPG buttons to render. The image will be rendered and automatically downloaded to your device.

Click here to see YouTube video demo


What book dimensions are supported?

Just select the type of book and after you upload your front cover image, the size and dimensions of the mesh will adjust to your design. You may need to adjust the thickness a bit :)


How do I download my mockup?

Once you are happy with the mockup you can export it in the EDIT menu. Just click on the "Render and Export" button and choose PNG or JPG. If you select PNG, the background will be transparent while the JPG will save the color of the background.


What is the resolution of the saved mockup and can I change it?

The resolution depends on the viewport size. It will be displayed below the export button.


What is Tone Mapping?

This is used to approximate the appearance of high dynamic range (HDR) on the low dynamic range medium of a standard computer monitor or mobile device's screen. The default is ACESFilmic. You can play with the settings until you get something you like. If you do not like this option, you can select "None" to disable it.


Can I save my scene and edit later?

It is not possible to save the scene yet. It will be added in the future updates


Do you store covers/images on the server?

NO. Mockupea works in your browser and all images that you upload are contained within your browser and never uploaded or sent to the server.


I have a question, who can I ask?

If you have any suggestions, questions or just want to say hi, feel free to send an email to mockupea@gmail.com :)
